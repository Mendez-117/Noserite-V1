About Noserite!



Noserite is a webpage developed by Aryan Khairnar. Moving your nose allows you to write or draw on the canvas. This was originally made by him in grade 6, to win an Inter-House competition. He came second. But, this webpage has a practical use as well. Disabled people may use this to communicate and engage in conversation with others. This is the main goal of the webpage to help people, who cannot speak, communicate. So that at the end of the day, their loved ones can tell them... "You can communicate!"
